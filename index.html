About Us

Hospitality Business Forum returns in 2026 for its second edition, a unique event in Romania, dedicated exclusively to the financial dimension of the hotel industry. We bring, for the first time, international speakers, investors, analysts, and experts who shape global hospitality trends.

The Forum addresses critical topics: investment strategies, capital attraction, profitable developments, asset management, and hotel exit scenarios. At a time when Romania is experiencing accelerated economic growth, HBF becomes the platform that connects the local industry with the real know-how of mature markets.

Our mission is to elevate the standard of conversation, deliver high-value insights rarely accessible in the region, and provide hospitality professionals in Romania with the tools needed to build hotels, brands, and destinations competitive at an international level.

HBF 2026: where Romania’s hospitality industry meets the expertise that can shape its future.
